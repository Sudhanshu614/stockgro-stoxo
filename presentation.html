Stream 01 · Human–AI Interaction Design · 26 years, one answer
26 years.
One answer.
From Horvitz 1999 through today's agentic AI — one convergent architecture for high-stakes tasks.
Convergent finding: conversation + persistent interactive artifact with direct manipulation. Not a preference — the validated architecture.
Graphologue — LLM Responses with Interactive Diagrams
Tier 1
Jiang et al. · ACM UIST 2023
Text-only LLM interfaces have three failure modes: verbosity, linearity, inflexibility.
Visual must stream simultaneously with text — not after.

CHI Best Paper

Research → Design · Decisions 1 & 2
The alternative
Return a text recommendation. Every existing product — Fuzz, SEVA, every robo-advisor.
Research demanded
Graphologue: three text-only failure modes apply directly to portfolio construction — verbosity, linearity, inflexibility. The basket card solves all three.
What we built
Basket card — a generated interactive artifact coexisting with the conversational response.
The alternative
Pending changes model — queue edits, batch approve, regenerate. Seriously considered.
Research demanded
DynaVis: editing confidence requires immediacy. The persistent widget must reflect the instruction in the same conversational turn. A pending queue breaks it.
What we built
Live rebuild — every confirmed instruction immediately updates basket and chip.
Research → Design · Decisions 3 & 4
The alternative
A richer chip — changelog of added/removed stocks, drift warnings inline.
Research demanded
DynaVis + Amershi G14: the chip answers one question — "where is my basket right now?" A changelog converts it from orientation device to decision surface. The thread's job, not the chip's.
What we built
Three-number chip — stocks · alpha vs Nifty · horizon. Permanent during edit. Invisible outside it.
The alternative
Open invest screen to returns projection. Every competitor does this. Numbers feel like information.
Research demanded
CFA 2025: explainability is the #2 barrier to AI adoption. Numbers at ₹30L activate the analytical brain — which second-guesses. Thesis activates conviction.
What we built
Thesis-first invest sheet — the rationale before any number. Ante-hoc interpretability by design.
